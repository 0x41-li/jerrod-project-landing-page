Helping for Eastern Washingtonians

We believe America is at its best when government intervention is at its least.

Eastern Washington Helpline is a website created to connect Washingtonians with information, services, and assistance, free from government control.

This site is run by our neighbors in Eastern Washington. Contact the helpline to:

  • a. Get the help you need.
  • b. Volunteer with the helpline.
  • c. Offer your experience to help others in Eastern Washington.
